The Project

Understanding campus sentiment is crucial for student well-being and engagement. UForecast provides a visual representation of student emotions across various campus locations, using a clickable SVG map with hover effects and dynamic pop-up reactions. For instance, selecting Richter Library may show grey skies to reflect a stressful environment, while the lake might have a tranquil atmosphere representing a relaxed vibe. Additionally, a side panel displays real-time weather updates for Coral Gables, FL, ensuring users stay informed about actual weather conditions alongside campus sentiment insights.
